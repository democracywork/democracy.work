Why Unsubscribe?

Every month, billions of dollars flow from consumers to a handful of tech giants. These companies hold outsized influence over the economy, policy, and daily life. This guide makes it easy to cancel those subscriptions and replace them with ethical alternatives.

How Consumer Boycotts Create Change

Recurring subscription revenue is the backbone of big tech. When enough people cancel at once, it shows up in quarterly earnings, triggers media coverage, and forces leadership to respond. Every month you stay subscribed is a vote of approval.

What About ICE Contracts?

Several consumer-facing companies maintain contracts worth tens of millions with U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement. By canceling services and switching to alternatives, consumers directly reduce the revenue that funds these operations.
